uTagGo Website

Project: Parking Lot

UI/UX design | RWD | Business Competition

uTagGo is an platform that integrate various services for drivers. Drivers can enter and exit the parking lot quickly by eTag , and automatically withhold payment.
uTagGo parking lot website contains all of parking infomations include maps, rates, real-time spaces and popular hours in Taiwan. Let users can find the best parking lot at their destination quickly and solve parking problems!
