uTagGo App

UI/UX design

uTagGo is an platform that integrate various services for drivers. Drivers can enter and exit the parking lot quickly by eTag , and automatically withhold payment.
uTagGo is an exclusive app for drivers that improves the driving experience. It provides automatically parking payment and eTag recharge services. You can also deserved points rewards when you park your car, and the points can be used to redeem ETC highway tolls for free!
